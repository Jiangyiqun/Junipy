The above graph is heatmap describing the linear correlation between features using all non-none records from 2000 to 2016. On the graph, PM25 would be the least important feature compared with others as the linear correlations between PM25 with other features are relatively low. The strongest relationship is population and Water Withdraw due to the linear correlation of 0.94. CO2 and WaterWithdrawals have high relationship with GDP which are higher than 0.75.
